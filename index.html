My Works

Great Gray Owl

In the warmth of summer, I pursued the enigmatic great gray owl. Through endless days in thick forests, the sun was my only compass, and its elusive silhouette my goal. The gentle rustling of leaves and distant calls only heightened my anticipation. Then, on a golden afternoon, our worlds collided. My lens focused, capturing not just an image, but a fleeting summer's enchantment.

Northern Rockies

Driving through the Canadian Rockies, nature's grandeur surrounded me. Towering peaks, azure lakes, and sprawling valleys painted an ever-changing canvas. At every curve, I'd pause, camera in hand, hoping to encapsulate the scene's magic. But photos, though beautiful, were mere echoes. The true essence was the journey itself, an intimate dance between wanderer and wilderness, forever imprinted on my soul.

The Great Horned Owlet

In the nearby park, whispers spread of a couple of owls. Intrigued, I ventured out one evening, seeking the fabled Great Horned Owlet. As the sun set, casting amber hues, its piercing eyes met mine amidst the shadows. My camera captured fleeting moments of this wild encounter. By nightfall, as the owlet disappeared, I was left with snapshots and a deep reverence for nature's nearby wonders.
